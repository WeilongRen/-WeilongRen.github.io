Biography

I obtained my Bachelor's degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University of Finance and Economics, Shandong, China, in June 2014. I obtained my Master's degrees from the School of Computing, Edinburgh Napier University, Edinburgh, U.K., in October 2016, and the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University of Finance and Economics, Shandong, China, in June 2017, respectively. I obtained my PhD degree at the Department of Computer Science, Kent State University in December, 2021, under the co-supervision of Dr. Xiang Lian and Dr. Kambiz Ghazinour. Starting from February 2022, I am a research scientist at Shenzhen Institute of Computing Sciences.
